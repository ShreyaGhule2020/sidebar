Aakash

Aakash PrimeClass is a live stream class that delivers education through New-Age Satellite Technology across India, thereby transforming learning and advancing education. The classes enable students to see, listen and participate in a lecture and interact with the faculty spontaneously and naturally as in a regular classroom. They can also raise queries, respond to polls, attempt multiple choice questions and assessments & mark attendance.
